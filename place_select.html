About Us

Circuitstyle is a free web-based building and fighting game inspired by early 2000s block games. Build structures, equip tools, and play with others — all from your browser.

No downloads. No installs. Just open the site and play.

Platforms we support:

Built with Three.js, PeerJS, and Firebase. Inspired by early Roblox.

New Players

Welcome to Circuitstyle! Here's how to get started:

  1. Sign up above with a username and password to save your progress.
  2. Click Play on Baseplate to join the game.
  3. Use the backpack to equip tools — blocks, swords, slingshots, and more.
  4. Press / or click the chat icon to chat with other players.
  5. Right-click to open the build menu and place blocks.

Your account is shared across all devices. Sign in anywhere to pick up where you left off.
